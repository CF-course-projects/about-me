About Me

Hello and welcome to my About Me page! Here I'll let you get to know me as a person where I'll share my background and interests. I speak English abd Italian fluently and have intermediate French, Spanish and basic Thai proficiency. After graduating I began to look into pivoting into software engineering thanks to a friend raving about the opportunity in the field and thus began my journey with Code Fellows. Working towards my future career in tech, my goal is to learn as much as I possibly can while also deepening my passion and hunger to create cool things that hopefully work as intended!
